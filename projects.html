GPS UGV 2.0

Microcontroller Project / May 2021


Test platform for GPS waypoint controller designed to optimize waypoint mission control. Currently mounted on DEERC RC truck (with swapped ESC and servo). Rocket Airbrake Mark1

Rocketry / March 2020


A basic flat-plate airbrake mechanism designed to induce drag via flat plates which extend out of a rocket. The flate-plate concept enables for simpler deceleration calculations as motor movement can be linearly correlated with crossectional area. The downside however, involves the unknown effect a flat plate design will have on rocket stability (specifically, it's CP). This mechanism was initially designed to function on the SEDS-Rocketry Division's Spaceport rocket to control rocket apogee. As of now, this design remains on the drawing board and is yet to be built.
